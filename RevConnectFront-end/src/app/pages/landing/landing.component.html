Help & Support

Need help with RevConnect? Our support team is here for you. Reach out at support@revconnect.app and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Common topics: account settings, privacy controls, reporting content, and managing your connections.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to everything we build. RevConnect collects only the data necessary to provide you a great experience — and we never sell it to third parties.

You have full control over your data. You can export, edit, or delete everything at any time from your account settings.

Terms of Service

By using RevConnect, you agree to share content responsibly and treat fellow members with respect. We have a zero tolerance policy for harassment, hate speech, and harmful content.

RevConnect reserves the right to remove content or accounts that violate community guidelines.
